Privacy Policy

Thank you for using Air Weather. We highly value your privacy and data security, and we do not collect or store any personal information.

How We Handle Data

  • Location Data: Used only to display real-time weather and air quality for your current location. It is never uploaded or stored on our servers.
  • City Settings: Your selected cities are stored locally on your device only.
  • Network Access: Used solely to fetch weather and air quality data from third-party APIs. No personal data is transmitted.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not collect personally identifiable information.
  • We do not share or sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not track user behavior.
